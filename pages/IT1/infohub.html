Victoria's Overseas-Born Population Insights

In 2021, 29.3% of Australians were born overseas, mainly from England, India, China, and New Zealand. Key trends include:

  • India: Growth from 31.14k in 2001 to 272.25k in 2021.
  • China: Increase from 38.86k in 2001 to 182.14k in 2021.
  • Vietnam: Fluctuating trend, reaching 99.35k in 2021.
  • Sri Lanka: Consistent rise to 72.1k in 2021.
  • Philippines: Steady growth to 71.25k in 2021.

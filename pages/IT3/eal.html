EAL Support

EAL: English as an additional language

It is a English literacy support program centraling around the learning and development.

Aims

1. Diverse Communication: Encourage participation in listening, speaking, reading, writing, and digital/visual communication.

2. Standard Australian English: Understand and apply the rules of Standard Australian English in various forms of communication.

3. Utilize English Creatively: Foster creative use of English for expression, information sharing, idea generation, engagement, entertainment, persuasion, and argumentation.

Benefits

1. Fundamental Language Skills:Building vital language and literacy skills for effective communication and comprehension.

2. Plurilingual Awareness: Cultivate understanding of how individuals use different languages and their significance in shaping identities within society.

3. Enhanced Communication and Cultural Understanding: Develop communication skills, linguistic knowledge, and cultural understanding to facilitate integration into Australian society.

Pathway Level
Pathway A (Year: Foundation ~ Year 2)
Pathway B (Year: Year 3 ~ Year 8)

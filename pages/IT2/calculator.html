Understanding Early Childhood Education

Discover Victoria's early childhood education system and its overall structure. Learn about kindergarten, preschool, and calculate the appropriate year to enroll your children.

Kindergarten / Early Learning

Starting Age: 3 - 4

  • Kindergarten are also known as kindy, pre-school, or early learning.
  • It is a structured learning program for children typically administered by qualified teaching professionals.
  • Children usually attend a four-year-old kindergarten program in the year prior to attending primary school, with some also completing three-year-old kindergarten programs.
  • Kindergarten programs are offered at various facilities, including kindergartens, childcare and early learning centers, and some private schools.
Primary School

Starting Age: 5-6

  • Primary school is the start of the formal school system and is compulsory for all students.
  • The first year of primary school is known by different names in each state. In Victoria it is known as prep.
  • Primary school runs for seven years, with prep or the first year of school essentially being year 0, followed by year 1 to 6.
  • After completing primary school, students enter high school for a further six years of schooling from grade 7 through to grade 12.

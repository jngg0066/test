What is Suburban Portal?

A Suburban Portal is an online platform or tool designed to provide information on suburban areas related to the selected country among the top five migrating into Victoria, Australia. It serves as a centralized hub where parents can view the surrounding schools and its details.

Monash LGA>> The Chinese community in Victoria is the second largest in Australia. Many people in the Chinese community live in Melbourne’s south–eastern and eastern suburbs. TheCity of Monash is home to one of the largest Chinese communities in Victoria with 47,238 people. The City of Whitehorse is next with 44,445 people. The Chinese community is an established and steadily growing community in Victoria, with 27% of the population arriving between 2016 and 2021.

Wyndham LGA>> The Indian community in Victoria is one of the largest in Australia. Many people in the Indian community live in Melbourne’s south–eastern and north–western suburbs. The City of Wyndham is home to one of the largest Indian communities in Victoria with 65,433 people. The City of Casey is next with 44,967 people. Between 2011 and 2021, the Indian community in Victoria continued to grow. The majority (55.3%) of the Indian community arrived in Victoria during this period.

The Filipino community in Victoria is the second largest in Australia. Many people in the Filipino community live in Melbourne’s south–western and south–eastern suburbs. The City of Wyndham is home to one of the largest Filipino communities in Victoria with 10,628 people. The City of Casey is next with 9,485 people. Between 1981 and 2016, the increase in the Philippines–born population of Victoria soared and went from 3,55 to 51,287 people. The Filipino community is growing, with many people arriving between 2016 and 2021.

Casey LGA>> The Sri Lankan community in Victoria is the largest in Australia. Many people in the Sri Lankan community live in Melbourne’s south–eastern suburbs. The City of Casey is home to one of the largest Sri Lankan communities in Victoria with 18,184 people. The City of Monash is next with 8,472 people. The Sri Lankan community is growing and many people arrived between 2001 and 2010.

Brimbank LGA>> The Vietnamese community in Victoria is one of the largest in Australia. Many people in the Vietnamese community live in Melbourne’s south–eastern and western suburbs. The City of Brimbank is home to one of the largest Vietnamese communities in Victoria with 32,861 people. The City of Greater Dandenong is next with 17,653 people. The Vietnamese community is well established in Victoria, with many arriving between 1981 and 1990.

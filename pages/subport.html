Monash LGA

  • The Chinese community in Victoria is the second largest in Australia.
  • Many people in the Chinese community live in Melbourne’s south–eastern and eastern suburbs.
  • The City of Monash is home to one of the largest Chinese communities in Victoria with 47,238 people.
  • The Chinese community is an established and steadily growing community in Victoria, with 27% of the population arriving between 2016 and 2021.

Wyndham LGA

Indian

  • The Indian community in Victoria is one of the largest in Australia.
  • Many people in the Indian community live in Melbourne’s south–eastern and north–western suburbs.
  • The City of Wyndham is home to one of the largest Indian communities in Victoria with 65,433 people.
  • The City of Casey is next with 44,967 people. Between 2011 and 2021, the Indian community in Victoria continued to grow. The majority (55.3%) of the Indian community arrived in Victoria during this period.

Filipino

  • The Filipino community in Victoria is the second largest in Australia.
  • Many people in the Filipino community live in Melbourne’s south–western and south–eastern suburbs.
  • The City of Wyndham is home to one of the largest Filipino communities in Victoria with 10,628 people.
  • The Filipino community is growing, with many people arriving between 2016 and 2021.

Casey LGA

  • The Sri Lankan community in Victoria is the largest in Australia.
  • Many people in the Sri Lankan community live in Melbourne’s south–eastern suburbs.
  • The City of Casey is home to one of the largest Chinese communities in Victoria with 18,184 people.
  • The Chinese community is growing and and many people arrived between 2001 and 2010.
